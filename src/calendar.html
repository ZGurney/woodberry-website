Our calendar of activity brings opportunities for learning and friendship to residents of all ages, and especially to youth looking to contribute to social change and personal development. The programmes include study and conversations, service projects in the community, sports, cultural activities, such as drama and crafts, suited to our local community.

Camps are also arranged each have term which last several days for youth and whole families. Through these activities, we strive to provide a safe and engaging environment for our community members to grow and learn together.

There are also many spaces and resources for parents, guardians, and educators and like minded organisations to help support the learning and development of children and youth.

Key dates for special events and weekly activities at the Redmond centre, as well as residential camps are included.
