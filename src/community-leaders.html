Community Leaders Programme

Training sessions and ongoing support for youth to develop understanding of their purpose and role in social transformation and community building.

Courses to Build Capacity for Service and Leadership

As a community leader, you and your friends form groups with younger peers and help them to explore important questions together, to consult, plan and carry out community service projects, and reflect on their actions as a group.

Youth find it reassuring to turn for advice to older peers who understand and sympathise, and can assist as true friends to develop. Our courses combine study with acts of service in the community.

They are intended to establish a dynamic pattern of action that will lead to the sound development of local communities, according to the interests and capabilities of the participants.

Materials and Approach

Training sessions and ongoing support

Assisting youth to develop a profound understanding of the purpose of their lives and their roles in contributing to social transformation and community building. Initial workshop include:

  • The role of youth in society and characteristics of their younger peers
  • Community building and offering mutual support
  • Contributing to the advancement of civilization

Educational Materials

The educational materials we have chosen to use are those of the junior youth programme and Ruhi institute as they are focused on building the qualities, attitudes, skills and knowledge needed to serve the younger generation and the community effectively.

The sources of knowledge upon which the books of the Institute draw are, on the one hand, the teachings of the Bahá'í Faith and, on the other, the accumulating experience of communities with aims like we have described above, that are dedicated to advancing the material and spiritual wellbeing of society.

"Regardless of their social situations, young people aspire for spiritual and intellectual growth and to make a contribution to the fortunes of humanity"
