What Are Devotional Gatherings?

A simple gathering of people from diverse faiths and backgrounds who come together in a locality to share in prayer, meditation and discussion of profound themes.

Often held in homes or neighbourhood centres, such gatherings are considered an essential practice of the spiritual wellbeing of a community, building bonds of friendship and following no set form.

These gatherings welcome people of all faiths and backgrounds, creating a space for spiritual connection and community building through shared reflection and prayer.
