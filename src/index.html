PROGRAMMES

Our programmes are dedicated to building a vibrant and integrated community life, engaging residents of all backgrounds and ages. They seek to contribute to unity and cohesion through the promotion of educational activities and projects of selfless service in the local area. Together, these create an environment for individuals and groups to develop. The Ruhi Institute conducts courses and educational programs for diverse age groups, from the tender age of 5 or 6 to adulthood. We draw on these programmes in Woodberry Down to contribute to the intellectual, moral and spiritual education of primary and secondary school students in the local area. In addition, training and support opportunities are extended to youth and adults in the community who wish to contribute to community building and social transformation.
